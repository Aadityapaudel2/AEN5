# Public Portal Release Surface

## Scope

This note records the March 2026 release-oriented restructuring of the public Athena portal.

## What Changed

- The landing page was restructured into a clearer information architecture rather than one long policy-heavy page.
- Full privacy and terms content now live on dedicated public routes.
- The landing page now explains where Athena, Evaluation, and SWARM land inside AEN.
- Institution onboarding is represented explicitly as a later layer rather than mixed into the live student-facing chat surface.
- The public runtime/prompt framing was strengthened around mathematics, tutoring, and teaching quality.

## Public Surface Structure

Landing page:
- hero
- capabilities/discover section
- architecture section
- mission section
- institution section
- trust cards linking out to legal pages

Dedicated legal pages:
- `/privacy`
- `/terms`

## Positioning

AthenaV5 is presented as part of the Artificial Evaluation Network.

- Athena: teaching and tutoring interface
- Evaluation: trust and verification layer
- SWARM: orchestration layer for specialist multi-step workflows

## Runtime Note

The public GUI config was cooled to reduce repetition risk and improve coherence for public release.
